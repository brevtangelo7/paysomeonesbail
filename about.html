About this site

An Independent Resource

Pay Someone's Bail is an independent, volunteer-built directory. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or financially connected to any of the organizations, bail funds, or attorneys listed or referenced on this site.

What this site is

  • A directory of non-profit ICE bond funds operating across the United States
  • A starting point for people who want to support detained immigrants
  • An educational resource about how ICE bonds work

What this site is not

  • An official service, legal resource, or government-affiliated site
  • Associated with any law firm, advocacy organization, or government agency
  • A guarantee of any fund's current activity, capacity, or legitimacy

Do your own research

Before donating to any organization, verify that it is currently active, in good standing, and accepting donations. Fund availability and capacity change frequently. We make no warranties about the accuracy or completeness of the information presented here.

Source data

Fund information is sourced primarily from the Community Justice Exchange National Bond Fund Network Directory. It may not reflect the most current status of any listed organization.
